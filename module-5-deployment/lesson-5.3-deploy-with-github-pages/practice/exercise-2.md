# Exercise 2: Share Your Game

## Objective

Create promotional materials and share your live poker game with friends, family, and the world!

## Instructions

### Part 1: Take Screenshots (10 minutes)

Capture images of your game for sharing:

#### Screenshot 1: Game Start
1. Load your live game
2. Click "Start Game"
3. Wait for cards to be dealt
4. Take a screenshot
   - Windows: Windows Key + Shift + S
   - Mac: Cmd + Shift + 4

5. Save as: `poker-game-screenshot-1.png`

#### Screenshot 2: Mid-Game Action
1. Play to the Flop or Turn
2. Show community cards and your hand
3. Take screenshot
4. Save as: `poker-game-screenshot-2.png`

#### Screenshot 3: Winning Hand
1. Play until you (or AI) win
2. Capture the winning hand announcement
3. Take screenshot
4. Save as: `poker-game-screenshot-3.png`

### Part 2: Add Screenshot to README (Optional - 15 minutes)

Make your repository more attractive:

1. **Upload screenshot to repository:**
   - Go to your repository
   - Create a folder called `screenshots/`
   - Upload your best screenshot
   - Commit: "Add game screenshot"

   - Click README.md
