How to Play

üéØ Objective

Win all the dealer's chips! Beat the dealer's hand to win the pot.

üéÆ Game Flow

  1. Both players ante 10 chips to the pot
  2. You receive 5 cards (dealer's cards stay hidden)
  3. Choose your action: Fold, Call, or Raise
  4. Dealer responds to your bet
  5. Cards are revealed - best hand wins the pot!

üÉè Hand Rankings (Best to Worst)

  1. Royal Flush - A-K-Q-J-10 same suit (best hand!)
  2. Straight Flush - 5 cards in a row, same suit
  3. Four of a Kind - 4 cards of same rank
  4. Full House - 3 of a kind + a pair
  5. Flush - 5 cards of same suit
  6. Straight - 5 cards in a row (any suit)
  7. Three of a Kind - 3 cards of same rank
  8. Two Pair - 2 different pairs
  9. One Pair - 2 cards of same rank
  10. High Card - When nothing else matches (worst hand)

üí∞ Betting Options

  • Fold: Give up this hand (lose your ante)
  • Call: Match the current bet and see the showdown
  • Raise: Bet more chips to increase the pot
    • Raise +10 chips
    • Raise +25 chips
    • Raise +50 chips

üé≤ Tips & Strategy

  • Play the odds: Strong hands (pair or better) are worth raising
  • Fold weak hands: High card or low pairs often lose
  • Bluff carefully: The dealer has AI logic - it's not easy to fool!
  • Manage your chips: Don't bet everything on one hand
  • Watch patterns: The dealer makes decisions based on hand strength
