How to Play Poker

Game Rules:

  • Objective: Get a better poker hand than the dealer
  • Starting Chips: You begin with 1000 chips
  • Ante: 10 chips to play each hand
  • Betting: You can Fold, Call, or Raise

Poker Hands (Best to Worst):

  • Royal Flush: A, K, Q, J, 10 all same suit
  • Straight Flush: Five cards in sequence, same suit
  • Four of a Kind: Four cards of same rank
  • Full House: Three of a kind + pair
  • Flush: Five cards of same suit
  • Straight: Five cards in sequence
  • Three of a Kind: Three cards of same rank
  • Two Pair: Two different pairs
  • One Pair: Two cards of same rank
  • High Card: Highest card wins

Betting Options:

  • Fold: Give up your hand and lose ante
  • Call: Match the current bet
  • Raise: Increase the bet amount
