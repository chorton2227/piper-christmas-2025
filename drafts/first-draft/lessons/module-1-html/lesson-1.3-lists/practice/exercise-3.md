## ðŸ“‹ Instructions

1. Create a new file called `guide.html`

3. Use ALL of these elements:
   - At least one unordered list
   - At least one ordered list

### Guide Topic Ideas:

- **How-To Guide**: How to play your favorite game, sport, or hobby
- **Beginner's Guide**: Introduction to something you love
- **Travel Guide**: Guide to your town, state, or dream destination
- **Care Guide**: How to take care of a pet, plant, or object
- **Learning Guide**: How to get started with a subject or skill
- **Survival Guide**: Tips for starting at a new school, surviving summer, etc.
