1. Create a new file called `my-story.html`
2. Write a short story (or tell about your day) using:
   - One `<h1>` for the story title
   - At least three `<h2>` tags for different chapters/sections
   - At least five `<p>` tags for story content
   - Some text formatting (`<strong>`, `<em>`, etc.)
   - At least one `<hr>` to divide sections

### Example Structure:
```
H1: The Adventure Begins
  H2: Chapter 1: The Forest
    Paragraph 1: Description
    Paragraph 2: What happened
  H2: Chapter 2: The Cave
    Paragraph 1: More story
    Paragraph 2: Exciting parts
  H2: Chapter 3: The Treasure
    Paragraph 1: The ending
```
