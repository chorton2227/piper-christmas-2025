My Favorite Colors

Blue is my absolute favorite color! It reminds me of the ocean and the clear sky on a sunny day. Blue makes me feel calm and peaceful.

Green is the color of nature and growth. I love seeing green trees and grass in the spring. It's such a fresh and lively color!

Pink is bright and cheerful! It's the color of flowers like roses and cherry blossoms. Pink always makes me smile.
