## Example: Pizza Shop Clicker

Here's a quick example to inspire you:

**Theme:** Pizza Shop  
**Click Object:** Giant pizza button  
**Per Click:** 1 pizza  

**Upgrades:**
1. Better Oven - 50 pizzas - +1 per click
2. Helper Chef - 100 pizzas - +1 pizza/second
3. Pizza Truck - 500 pizzas - +5 pizzas/second
4. Restaurant - 2,000 pizzas - +20 pizzas/second
5. Pizza Chain - 10,000 pizzas - +100 pizzas/second

**Win Condition:** Make 100,000 pizzas (Pizza Empire!)

**Rules:**
- Click the pizza to make pizzas
- Spend pizzas on upgrades
- Upgrades help you make pizzas faster
- Reach 100,000 pizzas to win!

**Screen Layout:** Pizza button in center, counter at top, shop on right
