# Exercise 3: Build a Blog Post

## üéØ Goal
Create a complete blog post using proper heading hierarchy and rich text formatting.

## üìã Instructions

1. Create a new file called `blog-post.html`
2. Write a blog post about ANY topic you're interested in
3. Use proper heading structure (h1 ‚Üí h2 ‚Üí h3)
4. Include all the formatting you've learned

### Required Elements:
- [ ] One `<h1>` - Blog post title
- [ ] At least 3 `<h2>` - Main sections
- [ ] At least 2 `<h3>` - Subsections under one h2
- [ ] At least 8 paragraphs
- [ ] `<strong>` for important points
- [ ] `<em>` for emphasis
- [ ] `<hr>` to separate sections
- [ ] `<br>` somewhere appropriate (quote, address, poem, etc.)
- [ ] At least 3 other formatting tags (`<mark>`, `<u>`, `<small>`, `<del>`)

### Example Structure:
```
H1: Why I Love Video Games
  H2: Introduction
    Paragraphs about the topic
  H2: My Favorite Games
    H3: Action Games
      Paragraph about action games
    H3: Puzzle Games
      Paragraph about puzzle games
    H3: Sports Games
      Paragraph about sports games
  H2: Why Gaming is Good
    Paragraphs with reasons
  H2: Conclusion
    Final thoughts
```

## ‚úÖ Success Check

Your blog post should:
- [ ] Have a clear hierarchy (h1 ‚Üí h2 ‚Üí h3)
- [ ] NOT skip heading levels
- [ ] Use only ONE `<h1>` tag
- [ ] Have well-organized content
- [ ] Use formatting purposefully (not randomly)
- [ ] Be at least 8 paragraphs long
- [ ] Include variety in formatting
- [ ] Look professional and easy to read

- Include an author byline with `<small>`

## üí° Blog Post Ideas

Pick a topic you love:
- **Reviews**: Favorite book, movie, game, toy
- **How-To**: Teach something you know
- **Opinion**: Your thoughts on something
- **Experience**: Tell about a trip or event
- **Comparison**: Compare two things
- **Top 5/10**: List your favorites with explanations
- **Day in the Life**: Describe a typical day
- **Dream**: Write about your dream job, house, etc.

## üìù Professional Formatting Tips

### Good Introduction:
```html
<h1>The Ultimate Guide to Making Friends</h1>
<p><em>By: Your Name | Date: November 17, 2025</em></p>

<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Making friends is one of the <strong>most important</strong> skills in life...</p>
```

### Good Section with Subsections:
```html
<h2>Ways to Meet New People</h2>
<p>There are many places where you can meet potential friends.</p>

<h3>At School</h3>
<p>School is a <mark>great place</mark> to make friends because...</p>

<h3>Through Hobbies</h3>
<p>Joining clubs or activities helps you meet people who share...</p>
```

### Good Conclusion:
```html
<hr>

<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>Remember, making friends takes time and <em>that's okay</em>! Just be yourself...</p>

<hr>

<p><small>Thanks for reading! Let me know what you think.</small></p>
```

## üéØ Self-Check Questions

After completing your blog post:
1. Does it have a clear structure?
2. Can someone quickly scan the headings to understand the content?
3. Did you use formatting to emphasize important points?
4. Does it look professional?
5. Is it interesting to read?
6. Would you want to share this with others?
