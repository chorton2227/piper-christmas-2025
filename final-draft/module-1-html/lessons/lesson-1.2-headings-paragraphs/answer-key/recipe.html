Delicious Chicken Casserole

This chicken casserole is a hearty and comforting dish that's perfect for family dinners. Follow the recipe below to make your own!

Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs (optional)

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, cooked rice, cream of mushroom soup, shredded cheddar cheese, milk, and frozen peas and carrots. Mix well.
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Pour the mixture into a greased casserole dish.
5. If desired, sprinkle bread crumbs on top for a crunchy topping.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the top is golden brown.
7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
