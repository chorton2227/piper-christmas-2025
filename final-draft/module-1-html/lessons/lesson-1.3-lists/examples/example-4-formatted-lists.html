Instructions: How to Study Effectively

  1. Step 1: Find a quiet place to study
    • Turn off TV and phone
    • Good lighting is important
    • Comfortable chair
  2. Step 2: Gather your materials
    • Textbooks
    • Notes
    • Pens and highlighters
  3. Step 3: Take breaks every 30 minutes
    • Stand up and stretch
    • Get a snack
    • Don't study for hours without breaks!
  4. Step 4: Review what you learned
    • Write down key points
    • Explain concepts out loud
    • Quiz yourself
