How to Make Hot Chocolate

  1. Heat 1 cup of milk in a pot (don't boil!)
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of sugar
  4. Stir until smooth
  5. Pour into a mug
  6. Add marshmallows on top
  7. Enjoy while warm!
