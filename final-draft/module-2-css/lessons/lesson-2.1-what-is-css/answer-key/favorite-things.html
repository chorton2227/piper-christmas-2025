My Favorite Things

Favorite Foods

I absolutely love pizza with lots of cheese! Ice cream is the best dessert, especially chocolate and vanilla swirl. Tacos are amazing too - so many delicious flavors in one bite!

Favorite Games

Minecraft is incredible - I love building and exploring! Roblox has so many fun games to play with friends. Animal Crossing is super relaxing and lets me design my own island paradise.

Favorite Colors

Blue reminds me of the ocean and the sky. Purple is royal and magical. Green is the color of nature and makes me think of forests and grass.

Favorite Movies

I love animated movies like Toy Story and Spider-Verse! Superhero movies are awesome too - Marvel and DC have the best action scenes.

Favorite Activities

Drawing and creating art is so much fun! I also enjoy reading adventure books, playing outside, and learning to code. Making websites is like creating art with code!
