What are Google Fonts?

Google Fonts is a free library of over 1,000 professional fonts you can use on your website. They're easy to add and make your site look polished and unique!

How to Use Google Fonts

  1. Visit fonts.google.com

    Browse the library and find fonts you like!

  2. Select your font

    Click "Get Font" then "Get embed code"

  3. Copy the link code

    Add it to your HTML <head> section

  4. Use in your CSS

    Apply the font-family in your styles!

Font Pairing Examples

Combine two fonts for professional-looking designs!

Playfair Display + Roboto

Elegant headings with clean, readable body text. This classic combination works well for blogs, portfolios, and professional websites. The serif heading adds sophistication while the sans-serif body ensures easy reading.

Montserrat + Open Sans

Modern and bold headings paired with friendly, approachable body text. Perfect for startups, tech companies, and contemporary designs. Both fonts share a clean, geometric style that creates unity.

Poppins + Poppins

Sometimes using the same font family with different weights works beautifully! Use bold for headings and regular for body text. This creates consistency and harmony in your design.
