Community Guidelines

To keep our community friendly and welcoming, we ask all members to follow these simple rules:

  1. Be Respectful - Treat everyone with kindness, regardless of skill level or gaming preferences
  2. No Hate Speech - We have zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment
  3. Keep it Clean - Use appropriate language suitable for all ages
  4. No Spam - Don't flood chat with repetitive messages or advertisements
  5. Help Each Other - Share tips, answer questions, and support fellow gamers
  6. Have Fun! - Remember, gaming is about enjoyment and community

Important: Members who violate these guidelines may be removed from the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is membership free?

Yes! ProGamer Hub community membership is completely free. We're gamers sharing our passion, not trying to make money.

Do I need to be good at games to join?

Absolutely not! Our community welcomes gamers of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, you'll fit right in.

What age do I need to be?

We recommend members be at least 10 years old. Members under 13 should have parental permission to join online communities.

How often do you post new content?

We aim to publish at least 2-3 new pieces of content each week, including reviews, guides, and news updates.

Can I contribute content?

Yes! We love featuring community content. Email us your ideas at progamerhub@email.com.
