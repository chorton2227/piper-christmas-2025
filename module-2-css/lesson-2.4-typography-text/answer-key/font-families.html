Serif vs Sans-Serif

Serif Fonts

Have decorative strokes (serifs) on the ends of letters. Traditional and formal. Good for print and long articles.

Sans-Serif Fonts

Clean and modern without decorative strokes. Excellent for screens and digital interfaces.
